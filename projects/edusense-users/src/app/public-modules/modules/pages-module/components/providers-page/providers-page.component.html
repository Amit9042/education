With EduSense, education institutes can easily conduct virtual classrooms, share study material with students, maintain attendance, organize exams and much more.

Conduct
live sessions

Teachers can conduct live sessions using our virtual classrooms having the capacity of up to 150 students.

EduSense is not only about virtual classrooms and sharing study materials. With EduSense the institutes can also organize the exams virtually.
